Saracens Rugby Union Football Club

Saracens Football Club are an English professional ruby union club based in London, England. After a year in the RFU Championship, they will return to Primership Rugby, the first tier of rugby in England, in 2021–22. Saracens have won 10 major trophies, they were European champions three times in 2016, 2017 and 2019. They have won Premiership Rugby five times, most recently in 2019, and won the domestic cup twice in 1998 and 2015. They have also won the second division title three times in 1989, 1995 and most recently won the RFU Championship in 2021, defeating Ealing Trailfinders Rugby Club in the championship playoff.
